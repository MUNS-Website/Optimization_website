Center of Excellence in Community Health Nursing

The Department of Public Health Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University is committed to being the center of knowledge and quality public health nursing practice. We integrate knowledge from research, academic service, and teaching to promote community health nursing practice and health outcomes.

The Center of Excellence focuses on human resource development in community health nursing, advancing practice, and driving health policy to promote community health nursing outcomes.
