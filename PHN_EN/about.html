About Us

Vision

The Department of Public Health Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University is the leader in community health nursing and the wisdom in health promotion for community and social well-being.

Mission

The Department of Public Health Nursing is responsible for organizing courses and teaching in undergraduate, graduate, and continuing education programs, conducting research, providing health care services for community and society, and promoting Thai culture. The curriculum mainly focuses on healthcare systems, family, and community nursing care.
