Mission & Vision
Core Mission of the Department

The Department of Obstetric and Gynaecological Nursing is committed to providing excellent and socially responsive midwifery and obstetric-gynaecological nursing education to undergraduate and graduate nursing students. The department continuously supports and develops faculty potential in academic instruction, research, and textbook writing. It delivers high-quality academic services to improve the quality of life of target populations and the general public, while fostering cultural awareness and preserving Thai arts and heritage.

Core Missions

Educational Excellence

Provides undergraduate and graduate nursing education, aiming for academic excellence and addressing social needs.

Research & Writing

Promotes continuous faculty research potential and the authorship of academic textbooks in obstetric-gynaecological nursing.

Quality Services

Improves and promotes the quality of life for target groups and the general public through standard nursing services.

Preserving Heritage

Promotes and develops a sense of responsibility for the preservation and conservation of valuable Thai arts and culture.
