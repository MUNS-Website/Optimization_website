Academic Services

The Department of Obstetric and Gynaecological Nursing has a policy to support faculty members in providing academic services concerning the health promotion of women, pregnant women, intrapartum and postpartum mothers, and newborns, to healthcare professionals and the general public. The department currently manages the following academic service projects:

Lady's Talk Project
Happy Family, Quality Pregnancy Project
Capacity Building Project for Nurses in Caring for Pregnant, Intrapartum, and Postpartum Women (Lao PDR)
Continuous Care System Development Project for Pregnant Adolescents by Primary Midwives
Sexual Health and Sex Education Awareness Project for Female Adolescents and Students
"Health Promotion for Women with Gestational Diabetes Integrated with Instruction" Project
