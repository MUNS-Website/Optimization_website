Core Missions

The Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing focuses on 4 main missions to drive the organization toward international excellence in nursing education and practice.

1. Education & Teaching

Provide mental health and psychiatric nursing courses for undergraduate and graduate students, cultivating academic excellence, moral integrity, and professional ethics.

2. Research & Innovation

Produce high-quality national and international research and academic work in mental health and psychiatric nursing.

3. Academic Services

Offer academic services to promote mental health for students, staff, and the public, collaborating with specialized institutes to train nurse specialists.

4. Arts and Culture Preservation

Preserve arts, culture, and the environment, promoting the integration of cultural dimensions in nursing practice and daily life to support well-being.
