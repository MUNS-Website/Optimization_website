Academic Services

The Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing is dedicated to translating our expertise and specialized knowledge into practical academic services for society, supporting mental health care and well-being of the public across all lifespans while building solid professional networks at both national and international levels.

Academic Service Pillars

1. Public Mental Health Promotion

  • Offer counseling programs to staff, students, and the general public.
  • Provide online consultation via Line Official Account "NS พลังใจ"
  • Contact via tel. 089-6861316 or the Department's Facebook page.

2. Research & Academic Consultant

  • Act as consultants for research and academic projects for external organizations.
  • Co-develop nursing practice guidelines for various hospitals.

3. Curriculum Development Network

  • Collaborate with Department of Mental Health (Galya Rajanagarindra, Somdet Chaopraya, Srithanya) to organize specialized training programs.
  • Collaborate with Yuwaprasart Waithayopathum Child Psychiatric Hospital to develop child and adolescent training curricula.

4. Specialized Short Courses

  • Organize psychology and psychiatric practical training for professional nurses.
  • Offer courses on mental health support for chronic illnesses and community psychiatry.
