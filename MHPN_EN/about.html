History of the Department

Welcome to the Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing

Mental health and psychiatric nursing is both an art and a science dedicated to promoting, preventing, treating, and rehabilitating the mental health of patients to achieve balance and well-being in their lives.

Originally established as the Psychiatric Nursing Unit under the Department of Medical and Psychiatric Nursing, it was later approved as an independent department within the Faculty of Nursing. Officially announced in the Royal Gazette on February 6, 1986, it became the 7th department of the Faculty of Nursing.
